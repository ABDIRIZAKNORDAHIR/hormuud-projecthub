ProjectHub — View on Phone (Mobile)
===================================

Your project ALREADY works on phones. Here is how YOU can see it,
and how STUDENTS see it on their phones.

OPTION 1 — Preview on your PC (no phone needed)
------------------------------------------------
1. Open http://localhost:5180/ in Chrome or Edge
2. Press F12 (Developer Tools)
3. Click the phone/tablet icon (Toggle device toolbar)
4. Choose a device: iPhone 14, Samsung Galaxy, etc.
5. Refresh the page

You will see the mobile layout:
- Stacked hero (photo + Hormuud University title)
- Full-width Sign in buttons
- Green Student / Teacher icons in top bar
- Portal cards one under another
- After login: bottom navigation bar (Home, Projects, Team…)

OPTION 2 — Real phone (same Wi-Fi)
----------------------------------
1. Run START.bat on your PC (server must stay on)
2. Find your PC IP: open CMD → ipconfig → IPv4 Address
   Example: 192.168.1.105
3. On the phone (same Wi-Fi), open Safari or Chrome:
   http://192.168.1.105:5180/

OPTION 3 — Install on phone like an app (PWA)
---------------------------------------------
1. Open the site on the phone (Option 2)
2. iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen
3. Android Chrome: Menu → Install app / Add to Home screen
4. ProjectHub opens full-screen like a native app

WHAT USERS SEE ON PHONE
-----------------------

HOMEPAGE (not signed in)
  - HU logo + ProjectHub header
  - Student & Teacher icon buttons + Sign In
  - Hero with campus photo and green branding
  - Large “Sign in as Student / Teacher” buttons
  - Portal cards (green student card, white teacher card)
  - Scroll sections: workflow, features, footer

STUDENT (after sign in)
  - Top: logo + search (compact header)
  - Dashboard with projects and progress
  - Bottom bar: Home | Projects | Team | Atlas | Settings
  - Floating + button for quick actions

TEACHER / ADMIN
  - Same bottom navigation (different menu items)
  - AI Tools where applicable

PHONE LINKS (replace YOUR_IP with your PC address)
--------------------------------------------------
  http://YOUR_IP:5180/
  http://YOUR_IP:5180/student
  http://YOUR_IP:5180/teacher

NOTES
-----
- Use portrait mode (vertical) for best layout
- SQL data is the same on phone and PC — one shared database
- Phone users need your server PC running and same Wi-Fi
